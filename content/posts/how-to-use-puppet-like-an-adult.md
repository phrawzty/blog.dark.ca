Hello friends,
Recently, [Ben Kero](https://twitter.com/bkero) (a fellow Mozillian) and I were invited to present a talk at [Linux Conf Australia](http://linux.conf.au/).  To say that we were excited about presenting at one of the best Libre / Open Source conferences in the world is an understatement.  We knew that we'd have to bring our A-game, and in all modesty, I like to think that we did.  If you were there in person, I'd like to personally thank you for coming out, and if you couldn't make it, that's ok - the organisers have made many videos from the 2013 LCA [available online](http://mirror.linux.org.au/linux.conf.au/2013/ogv/), including ours, entitled "[How to use Puppet like an Adult](http://mirror.linux.org.au/linux.conf.au/2013/ogv/How_to_use_Puppet_like_an_Adult.ogv)".
We cover a variety of topics, including parametrisation, how to select good pre-built modules, and how you can build eco-systems around Puppet itself.  Please feel free to drop us a line, either on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/phrawzty) or here on the blog.  Thanks !
