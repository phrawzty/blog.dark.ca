Hello everybody !
If you're interested in learning a little about Puppet at Mozilla, I highly recommend that you check out [Puppet Podcast #7](http://puppetlabs.com/puppet-labs-podcast/7/), where Brandan Burton ([@solarce](https://twitter.com/solarce)) and I ([@phrawzty](https://twitter.com/phrawzty)) talk with Puppet Labs' Mike Stahnke ([@stahnma](https://twitter.com/stahnma)) about just that topic.  It was our first podcast together, and frankly, it was more difficult that I thought it'd be.  That said, it was a great experience, and I hope to do it again sometime.  Hope you enjoy it !
